About the Extraterrestrial Endangered Species Protection Project

Extraterrestrial Endangered Species Preservation Project or EESPP was founded in December of 2018. We at EESPP believe that the rate of species lost due to massive interference of human development can be turned around by cryo-freezing the animals and sending them to a new space colony. By sending them to a newly-established space colony, the animals will be able to live free from the dangers of human poachers and trophy hunters and will have the opportunity to reproduce and grow the population of various endangered species.

EESPP works with brilliant minds from all over the world to further our goals. Nationally recognized biologists, veterinarians, zoologists, and astronauts work together to ensure optimum results and animal safety while undergoing the cryo-freezing process and making the journey to the new space colony.
